Article Submission Guidelines
  • Only submit publicly accessible articles from legitimate news sources.
  • Ensure the URL is correct and leads directly to the article.
  • Articles in languages other than English are accepted but may take longer to process.
  • Provide accurate information about the article to help our fact-checkers.
  • Submissions are reviewed by our community of fact-checkers.
